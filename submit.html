Submissions

Welcome to the Charles Carter submission page – we’re glad to see that you’re considering sending us your work! We charge no fee for submission, read all work submitted to us, and provide detailed feedback on everything we read whether it is accepted or rejected. We only ask that you abide by our guidelines of submission; if you do not we unfortunately cannot guarantee your piece will be read simply due to the high volume of submissions we receive.

If you are sure that your work is typo free and formatted correctly please submit it in the appropriate section below!

Writing

Thank you for considering a submission to our publication. We appreciate your continued interest and support. If you’re wondering whether you should submit or not, wonder no more. Submit.

We don’t care if you write short stories, poetry, non fiction, medieval sonnets, or Doctor Who fanfic. If it’s written, and people want to read it, we want it in our submission box. So if this is your first time submitting to a publication, don’t let things like being “literary” or “good” stress you out. Give it a whirl! We also accept reprints, so if you’ve got published work you’d like to see published again, have at it. Just click submit. And remember, whether your piece is accepted or rejected, we will send you back a list of detailed feedback!

Guidelines

If your work exceeds 5,000 words please submit using the Long Piece submission form. This ensures that your work is read in a more timely manner and reduces confusion for the coordinators within Charles Carter. Please follow these guidelines to ensure we are able to read your piece: if the format differs too greatly from what we ask for we will unfortunately be unable to consider your submission for publication simply due to the large volume of works we receive.

  • Submit as PDF
  • Number the pages
  • If submitting a collection, title the collection using a cover page in the pdf with the collection title centered
  • If submitting multiple separate poems, submit using individual forms for each single poem
  • Please use 12 pt. standard serif or sans-serif fonts
  • Do not include the author’s name or contact information or document name in the document to ensure anonymity during the review process. If you do not do this we cannot guarantee anonymity during the review.

Artwork

Thank you for considering a submission to our publication. We appreciate your continued interest and support. If you’re wondering whether you should submit or not, wonder no more. Submit.

Here, we accept submissions of original art ranging from graphic design, to illustration, to photography. As long as it’s in image format we are glad to review it! Our only request is that you avoid excessively lewd imagery or copyrighted subjects. Artwork we accept may be paired with written work appearing in our online anthology or one of our issues.

Guidelines

The names of the files will be logged as the names of the artwork. We accept up to 5 submissions here. Please make sure that the files are formatted for typical web standards:

  • 300ppi
  • in a lossless file type like .png
